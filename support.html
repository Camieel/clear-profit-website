Getting started

How do I install ClearProfit?

Install from the Shopify App Store. ClearProfit will sync your product costs from Shopify automatically on first install. The onboarding wizard walks you through setting up transaction fees, alert thresholds, and fulfillment hold settings.

How long does the initial setup take?

About 5 minutes. The onboarding wizard guides you through every step: COGS check, transaction fees, margin thresholds, and ad integrations.

Where does ClearProfit get my product costs?

From Shopify's native "Cost per item" field on every product variant. If you've already filled in these fields in Shopify, ClearProfit syncs them automatically on install. You can also override costs manually or upload them via CSV on the Products page.

Fulfillment holds

How does the automatic fulfillment hold work?

When an order comes in, ClearProfit calculates the net profit in real time. If the margin falls below your configured threshold, it places a hold on the fulfillment order via Shopify's API — before the order is shipped. You can release or cancel the hold from the dashboard.

How do I set the hold threshold?

Go to Settings → Fulfillment Hold and enter a margin percentage. For example, setting 10% means any order with a net margin below 10% will be held automatically. Set to 0 to only hold orders that are actually losing money.

Can I release a held order?

Yes. Go to the Dashboard, find the order under "Held Orders", and click Release. The fulfillment hold is removed and Shopify can proceed with shipping.

What if the hold doesn't apply in time?

ClearProfit processes the order webhook within seconds of the order being placed. For most stores, the hold is applied before the fulfillment system picks it up. For very fast fulfillment workflows, contact us to discuss your setup.

COGS & profit calculation

What costs are included in the profit calculation?

Revenue minus: COGS (cost of goods), transaction fees (% + fixed), default shipping cost, allocated ad spend for the day, and discounts. All configurable in Settings.

What happens if a product is missing a cost price?

The variant is counted as €0 COGS in the calculation, which inflates profit. ClearProfit marks these orders as "Incomplete" and shows a banner on the dashboard with a count of variants missing cost data. Fix them on the Products page.

Can I override the COGS for a specific variant?

Yes. Go to Products, find the variant, and enter a custom cost. This override takes priority over the Shopify-synced cost and won't be overwritten by future Shopify updates.

Ad integrations

How does ad spend allocation work?

ClearProfit pulls your total daily ad spend from Meta, Google, and TikTok. It then allocates that spend proportionally across all orders for that day, based on each order's share of daily revenue. So if your daily revenue is $1,000 and an order is worth $100, it gets 10% of that day's ad spend.

How do I connect Meta Ads?

Go to Settings → Ad Integrations → Connect Meta Ads. You'll be redirected to Facebook to authorize the connection. Select your ad account and you're done. ClearProfit will sync the last 30 days of spend automatically.

Can I sync ad spend manually?

Yes. In Settings → Ad Integrations, each connected platform has a "Sync now" button that pulls the latest 30 days of spend data immediately.

Shopify Flow

How do I use ClearProfit with Shopify Flow?

ClearProfit adds a Flow trigger called "Order profit calculated" that fires after every order. It exposes fields like margin percentage, net profit, and whether the order is a loss. You can use these to build custom automations — for example, sending a Slack notification when margin drops below 5%.

Does ClearProfit have a Flow action too?

Yes. There's a "Hold order for review" Flow action that lets you trigger a fulfillment hold from any Flow workflow, not just margin-based ones.

Billing & cancellation

Can I cancel my subscription?

Yes, at any time. Go to Shopify admin → Settings → Billing → Apps, find ClearProfit, and cancel. No notice period required.

Will my data be deleted if I uninstall?

Your data is stored securely. If you reinstall within 48 hours, all data will be intact. After 48 hours, data is permanently deleted in compliance with Shopify's GDPR requirements.
