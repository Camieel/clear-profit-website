Privacy Policy

Last updated: 16 April 2026

ProfitGuard is a Shopify app built by Untapped B.V. (Amsterdam, Netherlands). This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights under GDPR and applicable privacy laws.

1. Who we are

ProfitGuard is operated by Untapped B.V., registered in the Netherlands. Contact us at info@untapped.nl.

2. What data we collect

When you install ProfitGuard on your Shopify store, we collect and store the following:

  • Store information: Your Shopify store domain and access tokens required to operate the app.
  • Order data: Order ID, revenue, discounts, line items, and fulfillment status — used to calculate per-order profit.
  • Product and variant data: Product titles, variant IDs, and cost-per-item values — used for COGS calculation.
  • Ad spend data: Aggregated daily spend, impressions, and clicks from connected ad platforms (Meta, Google, TikTok) — used to allocate ad costs per order.
  • Settings and configuration: Your fee settings, margin thresholds, alert email addresses, and expense data.

We do not collect: customer personal data (names, email addresses, shipping addresses), payment information, or any data beyond what is necessary to operate the profit tracking functionality.

3. Ad platform integrations

If you connect Meta Ads, Google Ads, or TikTok Ads, we store OAuth access tokens and the ad account ID you select. These tokens are used solely to pull aggregated daily spend data. We do not access campaign creative, audience data, or personal information of your ad platform users.

4. How we use your data

We use your data solely to: calculate real-time profit on every incoming order, trigger and manage fulfillment holds via Shopify's API, allocate daily ad spend proportionally across orders, send alert emails when orders fall below your margin threshold, send weekly P&L summaries, display profit trends in your dashboard, and manage your subscription via Shopify Billing.

We do not sell, rent, or share your data with third parties for marketing or any other purposes.

5. Data storage and security

Your data is stored in a PostgreSQL database hosted on Neon (AWS eu-central-1, Frankfurt). All connections use TLS encryption. The app is hosted on Railway with HTTPS enforced. We implement appropriate technical and organisational measures to protect your data against unauthorised access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction.

6. Data retention

Data is retained for as long as the app is installed on your store. When you uninstall ProfitGuard, all store data is permanently deleted within 48 hours, in compliance with Shopify's mandatory data deletion requirements.

7. GDPR compliance

As a Netherlands-based company, we comply fully with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR). Under GDPR, you have the right to access, correct, delete, and object to the processing of your data. To exercise any of these rights, contact us at info@untapped.nl.

8. Shopify data requests

  • Customer data request: ProfitGuard does not store customer personal data. We store order IDs and revenue figures only, not names, addresses, or emails.
  • Customer data erasure: We respond to customer redact requests within 30 days. As we don't store personal customer data, there is typically nothing to delete.
  • Shop data erasure: All store data is deleted within 48 hours of uninstallation.

9. Cookies

ProfitGuard operates entirely within the Shopify Admin embedded app environment and does not use cookies on this website (profitguard.nl) for tracking or analytics.

10. Third-party sub-processors

  • Railway: App hosting — United States
  • Neon: Database hosting — AWS eu-central-1, Frankfurt
  • Shopify: Authentication and billing
  • Resend: Transactional email delivery (alert emails and weekly P&L summaries)
  • Meta, Google, TikTok: Ad spend data (only when you connect these integrations)

11. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify merchants of significant changes via the app. The current version is always available at profitguard.nl/privacy.html.
