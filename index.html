How it works

Order in. Profit checked.
Loss stopped. In seconds.

Fully automatic. No manual work, no CSV uploads, no checking dashboards on Monday morning.

Step 01
📦

Order placed

Customer checks out. ProfitGuard picks it up via Shopify's order webhook — instantly.

Step 02
🧮

Profit calculated

Revenue minus COGS, transaction fees, shipping, discounts, and proportional ad spend. Under 2 seconds.

Step 03
⚖️

Threshold checked

Margin above your threshold? Ship it. Below? Fulfillment is automatically held and you get an email alert immediately.

Step 04
✅

You decide

Review held orders in the dashboard. Release or cancel in one click — full cost breakdown included.

Action Center

Not a dashboard.
A decision engine.

Every time you open ProfitGuard, it tells you exactly what to fix — sorted by profit impact. No interpretation required.

📊

Proportional loss attribution

Ads, COGS, shipping, and fees are weighted by how much they contributed to each loss. You see which cost actually caused the problem.

🎯

Product-level root cause

ProfitGuard identifies which product drove the most losses and which cost is dominant. "Jordan 1 Retro — mainly COGS (97%)" in one line.

⚡

Worst first, always

Every list is sorted by loss impact. You see the biggest problem first, not in chronological order.

💰

"Recover ~$X/week if fixed"

Every action item shows the recoverable amount — a real dollar figure tied to a specific fix, not an abstract percentage.

Features

Everything you need.
Nothing you don't.

Built for Shopify merchants doing $20K–$500K/month who've outgrown spreadsheets but don't need a $200/month enterprise dashboard.

Only in ProfitGuard🛑

Automatic fulfillment holds

No competitor offers this. Orders below your margin threshold are held before they ship. One-click release or cancel from your dashboard with full cost breakdown.

Only in ProfitGuard🎯

Loss attribution engine

Identifies which product is losing money and which cost — ads, COGS, shipping, or fees — is dominant. Proportional attribution ranked by impact, not just totals.

Only in ProfitGuard⚡

Shopify Flow connector

Build profit-based automations in Shopify Flow. Trigger on margin below threshold. Take any action Flow supports: hold, tag, notify your team, and more.

📊

Real-time profit per order

Every order shows revenue, COGS, transaction fees, shipping, discounts, and ad spend — calculated via webhook the moment it comes in. Refunds auto-adjust immediately.

📢

Meta, Google & TikTok Ads

Connect via OAuth. Daily ad spend allocated proportionally across orders by revenue share. See the true profit of every sale including ad cost attribution.

📦

COGS management

Auto-syncs from Shopify's cost fields. Manual override per variant (preserved on resync). Bulk CSV import. Missing COGS audit with estimated profit impact.

💸

Discount & refund tracking

Every discount shown as a cost line on every order. Stacked promotions and leaked codes caught in real time. Refunds and cancellations auto-adjust order profit.

🔔

Email alerts + weekly P&L

Instant email when an order falls below your threshold. Plus a weekly P&L summary every Monday morning with top losses and recoverable amounts.

🗓

Recurring expenses

Add subscriptions, warehouse rent, agency fees. Pro-rated daily into your P&L so net profit is always the real number — not just order-level margin.
